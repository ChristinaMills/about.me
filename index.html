About Me

I was born in England, at Lakenheath Air Force Base. At age of 2, moved to the US. Living at various Air Force bases. My family settled in Tucson, AZ when I was 6 years old. Tucson is where I grew up and currently live.

I have spent the last 30 years working in many different forms of retail.I went to school for Fashion Design and Merchandizing, while working in a grocery store. I have been working in the fashion industry for 20 years, managing my own store for the last 16 years. At the end of 2016, my store closed by the company. I realised that this was an opportunity to get into a different industry, one that has a stable and growing future.
